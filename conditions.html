To send a tender to Africa Trade Forum implies explicitly the sender agrees to pay a success fee to SCAC Network for any sale done to a customer from our web sites if SCAC business services assistance is provided. Success fees rates displayed on Africa Trade Forum are exclusive of taxes (applicable for deals in Canada only) When a transaction is completed, the seller owes Africa Trade Forum / SCAC Network a "Success Fee" for our matchmaking service. This commission (excluding taxes, if any, and bank fees) is calculated as a nominal percentage of the whole amount of the transaction (FOB basis only), as shown in the table below:
