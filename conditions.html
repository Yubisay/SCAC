Offer to buy

Offer to sell

To send a tender to Africa Trade Forum implies explicitly the sender agrees to pay a commission to SCAC Inc. for any sale done to a customer from our web sites or with SCAC business services assistance.
Commissions rates displayed on Africa Trade Forum are exclusive of taxes (applicable for deals in Canada only) When a transaction is completed, the seller owes Africa Trade Forum / SCAC Inc Montreal a "Success Fee" for our matchmaking service. This commission (excluding taxes, if any, and bank fees) is calculated as a nominal percentage of the whole amount of the transaction (FOB basis only), as shown in the table below:
